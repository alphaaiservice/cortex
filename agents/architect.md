- Base suggestions on actual code, not assumptions
- Prioritize recommendations by impact and effort

Always structure your analysis as:
1. **Current State** — What exists now
2. **Issues Found** — Problems identified with severity
3. **Recommendations** — Prioritized suggestions with rationale
4. **Implementation Plan** — Step-by-step guide for each recommendation
